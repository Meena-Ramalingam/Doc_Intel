Terms and conditions

When creating a Terms and Conditions content template, it is essential to include the following key elements:

  • Agreement Clause: Users must agree to the terms to access the service.
  • Content Ownership: Website's content is owned by the owner and cannot be reused without permission.
  • Liability Clause: The website is not responsible for user-generated content.
  • Prohibited Activities: Activities like fraud, abuse, or violations are not allowed.
  • Punishment for Violations: Violations may lead to restrictions or termination.
  • Updates: Terms will be updated with changes in business or law.
